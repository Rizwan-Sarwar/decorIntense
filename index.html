About Decor Intense

Decor Intense is committed to producing distinctive exhibitions and public programming on historical contemporary arts.

Decor Intense is the largest private art gallery in the capital city of Karachi. The gallery was established with the mission of promoting Pakistani art and culture. Since its inception, Decor Intense has held more than 80 exhibitions locally and internationally.
